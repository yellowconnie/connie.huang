Flow


7"*3"

oil on woodboard

2022



The proliferation of the internet and the use of digital media has infiltrated every aspect of our lives. Painting today is no longer looked at within the confines of antiquated tradition but is instead seen and read within the contemporary moment of artistic production which includes the aid of digital tools and the influence of social media. Today, it is rare to find an artwork that is not somehow mediated by the internet which has the ability to proliferate, disperse and transmit images. But how does painting fit into all of this? If painting requires face-to-face interaction, how does it advocate for itself in the technological sphere?.

I created this painting with one of the oil painting techniques called glazing. And created this glowy and reflecting work, which is hard to document and hard to present it on internet. The intensions is to hope people can spend time and enjoy, observe it in-person, face to face, feel every details in the artwork.
