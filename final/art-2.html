A Walk in the Clouds


24"*30"

acrylic on canvas

2022



Based on the song Dream by John Cage, a piano piece. It gave me a feeling of walking in the woods and some sunlight filter through the trees and somehow it's like walking in the clouds. I want to demonstrate the soft, peaceful, quiet, dreamy mood that the song gave me. So the colors I chose are mostly earth tone color. Also because the song is mostly high pitch I chose these pretty light colors. And the dark green and that kinda grey and green color remains the low pitch part.
